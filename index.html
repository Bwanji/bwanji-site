Digital Church

By Larry Mwansa

What makes a Digital-Only Church? Simply put, it utilizes digital tools to create a church in existing digital communities. The intent of a Digital-Only Church is not to launch in physical space, but to utilize digital tools to reach, engage, encourage, and disciple people to be the church digitally and physically.

The idea is not to “do” church in a building but to BE the church wherever God takes you. A Digital-Only Church is not a virtual building where people meet, but a digital distribution network allowing the Gospel to spread. This is where technology, relationships and disciple-making converge towards creating a movement of God, digitally.

This is about empowering people to use digital tools to to connect, engage and disciple. Think of it this way it like an outreach strategy but instead of your neighbourhood or your city, we doing this is digital and metaverse spaces.

We work with churches and individuals to o create micro-networks within the organization. Through these micro-networks, participants will be grouped into similar approaches:

- Digital Churches (Social Media Churches, Streamer Churches) - Metaverse Churches - Physical Churches doing Digital Ministry Redefining Power Power has changed. The old model of “power” thrives on control. Nothing kills a movement like control. The new model of “power” is less about control, and much more about Empowering. Equipping. Platforming. Releasing. This is how today’s movements happe.
